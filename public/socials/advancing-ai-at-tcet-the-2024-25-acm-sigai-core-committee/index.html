Advancing AI at TCET: The 2024-25 ACM SIGAI Core Committee

Engineering the Future of AI

The TCET ACM SIGAI community enters a new chapter for the 2024-25 academic year, driven by a commitment to technological exploration and academic excellence. This newly appointed committee represents a diverse group of student leaders poised to push the boundaries of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML).

The chapter operates under the strategic mentorship of faculty members who provide the foundational support for the community's growth and academic integrity.

Vision and Community Engagement

This team embodies the essence of innovation and determination. With a focused mission to cultivate a thriving AI ecosystem, the committee is prepared to lead high-impact workshops, technical events, and research-oriented initiatives.

Under this new leadership, TCET ACM SIGAI aims to inspire students across all disciplines to explore the limitless possibilities of modern technology. Students are encouraged to participate, share ideas, and contribute to the upcoming initiatives designed to foster growth in the evolving landscape of AI and ML.
