Building Agentic AI and RAG Workflows: A Consultancy Engagement with CREO AI

Project Overview

In collaboration with the Department of AI & ML, I recently concluded a specialized consultancy engagement with CREO AI Pvt. Ltd. The primary objective was to architect and deploy a custom voice-based chatbot designed to meet specific internal organizational requirements.

The solution leveraged Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Agentic AI frameworks, transforming a standard conversational interface into a sophisticated system capable of executing complex, multi-step tasks with high precision.

Technical Implementation & Core Contributions

Throughout this engagement, our team maintained full development ownership of the product. My work focused on bridging the gap between theoretical AI models and production-ready systems, ensuring that every component was optimized for performance and reliability.

Key areas of contribution included:

* Workflow Design: Architecting end-to-end chatbot workflows to manage state, context, and voice-to-text transitions seamlessly.
* RAG Pipeline Engineering: Integrating robust RAG pipelines with internal API endpoints to facilitate accurate, data-backed responses.
* Agentic Automation: Building core components of the agentic system, enabling the chatbot to autonomously interface with provided tools and documentation.
* Integration & Testing: Ensuring seamless integration with existing internal infrastructure and conducting rigorous performance optimization.

Industrial Impact and Key Takeaways

This collaboration provided significant exposure to the practicalities of applied RAG engineering and the nuances of building Agentic AI systems. Operating under a formal affiliation agreement provided our team with direct access to proprietary APIs and professional toolsets, offering a realistic view of how modern AI products are built and scaled within a corporate environment.

I am grateful for the guidance of Abhishek Bhate (Founder, CREO AI) and Dr. Shiwani Gupta (HOD AI&ML), and for the partnership of my teammate Ayush Rai. Their direction was instrumental in navigating the technical challenges of this project.

Future Directions

As I transition into my winter internship and subsequent AIML research, the foundations I have strengthened in workflow automation and system architecture will serve as a vital springboard. I look forward to applying these insights to increasingly complex challenges in the field of intelligent systems.
